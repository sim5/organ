Organ Delivery

  • High Concept: Dodging traffic and deers to safely deliver the organ to the hospital
  • Player Experience Goals: Tension to avoid crashing into cars and deers. Urgency to deliver the organs to the waiting recipient.
  • Genre: Racing.
  • Game play: Use the arrow keys to move and dodge deers and cars.
  • Prototype goal: It is a dodging game.
  • Features: Tons of cars and deers approaching.
  • Setting/Premise: The road to the hospital is full of deers and massive traffic.
  • Story: There is an organ recipient in the hospital with very serious conditions and the ambulance has to deliver the organs as soon as possible.
  • Target Audience: All ages.
  • Play time: Maximum 50 seconds.
  • Strategies: You should watch out for the deers and cars at the same time. It is better to stay on the right lanes since the cars on the right lanes move in the same direction.
